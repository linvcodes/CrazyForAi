## CHARACTER BIBLE
Keep these locked across every prompt so the world stays consistent.

**KAI** — the main character. Based directly on the reference avatar: a young woman, mid-20s, with black shoulder-length hair — slightly wavy, messy layered cut with blunt bangs and face-framing pieces. Big round tortoiseshell glasses. Bright eyes with expressive anime-style rendering. Septum nose ring. Small gold hoop earrings. Thin choker necklace. Dark/black nail polish. Oversized zip-up hoodie in cyan #14a4c6 (the dark green hoodie from the reference, recolored to this exact cyan). Energetic, chaotic, fun — her default expression is a half-grin with tongue peeking out or a wink, peace sign optional. She always has a laptop somewhere nearby. The vibe is: indie tech girl who can break and fix anything, zero impostor syndrome.

**CHATGPT** — a compact companion robot, roughly shoulder-height to Kai. Body is smooth rounded pearl-white with subtle cool-grey panel joints. Its face is a slightly convex oval screen that shows large expressive anime-style eyes and a curved line mouth — happy, curious, calm. The **ChatGPT logo** (the black-and-white swirl/sparkle symbol) glows softly on its chest panel in white with a faint green-white inner light. Arms are smooth rounded cylinders ending in flat open hands. It hovers a few centimeters off the ground at all times. When thinking, a small holographic speech bubble with monospace text floats beside its head. Personality: wise, patient, slightly professorial — the planner of the group.

**REPLIT** — a second companion robot, slightly shorter and more compact than ChatGPT. Body is warm white with glowing orange-amber (#FF7B3A) accent trim on its panel edges and vents. The **Replit logo** (the three-triangle geometric R shape) glows on its chest in orange-amber. Face is a rectangular screen showing more kinetic anime expressions — wide excited eyes, big grin, speed-lines around it when it's in build mode. Arms end in little magnetic claw attachments. Always mid-action: carrying parts, assembling things, sparks flying. A pink (#FE5E76) blinking LED on its shoulder like a status light. Personality: hyperactive builder, never sits still, gets genuinely thrilled when something compiles.

**Art style for all scenes:**
- Semi-realistic anime digital painting — Genshin Impact / Blue Archive / Honkai Star Rail quality
- NOT 3D render, NOT Pixar, NOT flat vector or cel-shading with thick outlines
- Characters rendered with: detailed hair with individual strand clusters and light sheen, subsurface scattering warmth on skin, cloth folds and fabric texture on the hoodie, expressive large anime eyes
- Dramatic volumetric atmospheric lighting — strong colored rim lights, neon glow spill from emissive elements, light fog/bloom in the atmosphere
- Depth of field: characters sharp and detailed, backgrounds soft painterly bokeh
- Robots rendered with: smooth plastic sheen, specular highlights on curved surfaces, soft emissive glow from logo panels and screen faces, ambient occlusion in panel seams
- Floating UI text cards: frosted glass panels, white at ~88% opacity with 2px bright white edge, catching environment light with a subtle specular sheen — they feel physical and present in the scene
- All card text: Rubik font, dark navy #0d2233
- Pink (#FE5E76) for energy — spark trails, arrow glows, emissive accents
- Yellow (#FFD72A) for idea moments — discovery sparks, lightbulb reveals
- Cyan (#29C6DC) rim lights from behind on all characters
- Background atmosphere always built on cyan #14a4c6 as base
- No stock character presets, no AI-default face generation, no Facebook/Bitmoji energy
